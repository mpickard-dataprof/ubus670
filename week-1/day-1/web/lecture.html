Welcome to Day 1. Today we explore what Generative AI actually is, how it works under the hood, and why it matters for business.
Four objectives: Explain LLMs conceptually, Define tokens/context/temperature, Identify hallucination risks, Navigate Google Gemini.
Beacon Retail Group: regional chain founded 1987, 25 stores, 1200 employees, $312M revenue. Competing with Amazon, Walmart, Target. Students are the AI Strategy Task Force advising CEO Pat Holloway.
HR: 4200 apps/year, 6 weeks to screen (Volume). Marketing: 850 emails/week, 60% routine (Repetition). Finance: 1200 reports/month, 4% errors (Accuracy). Can AI help? Let's find out.
Three eras: Traditional software follows rules, ML finds patterns in data, GenAI creates novel content from prompts. The key shift is from recognition to creation.
Not a database retrieval — a prediction. AI synthesizes new content from learned patterns. Think of it as an extremely well-read assistant writing new content based on billions of documents.
LLM definition: deep learning model trained on massive text to generate human-like language. Scale: 300B+ training words, 1T+ parameters. A human reads ~1B words in a lifetime.
1 token ≈ 0.75 words (4 characters). Beacon email case: 850 emails/week × 470 tokens = 1.6M tokens/month. Gemini Pro: ~$2/month vs GPT-4: ~$47/month. Efficiency saves money.
Tokenizer breaks text into tokens, Embedder converts each token into a vector of numbers. "Unicorn" becomes [0.9, 0.1, 0.5...]. This math represents meaning.
Words cluster by meaning in vector space: Food words together, royalty words together, service sentiment words together. For Beacon: "This service is trash" is mathematically close to "I want a refund" — enabling semantic search.
Same word "bank" means different things: "bank was crowded...ATM" = financial institution. "bank was muddy...boat" = river edge. Attention mechanism connects all words simultaneously to resolve ambiguity.
LLMs predict the next token like a slot machine: "revenue..." → increased (34%), decreased (28%), grew (15%), fell (12%). Temperature dial: Low (0.1) = precise/factual, High (0.9) = creative/random. For Beacon expense reports: use low temperature.
Context window includes: your prompt, uploaded PDFs, and chat history. NO memory beyond current session. GPT-4 Turbo: 128K tokens (~1 novel). Gemini 1.5 Pro: 2M tokens (~20 novels). Larger context = higher cost.

Checkpoint: Managing Memory

B) Gemini deleted the data for privacy

Correct! Each new chat starts with a blank context window. The AI has no memory of previous sessions. Always re-upload documents in a new chat.

Engagement check: Fiction (Gemini remembers PDFs from last session — context resets), Fact (lowering temperature reduces hallucination risk), Fiction (200 words ≠ 200 tokens — 1 token ≈ 0.75 words).
Anatomy of a Lie: AI doesn't know Beacon's CEO, but CEO names "usually sound like" certain patterns → generates "James Robertson" as a statistically plausible but fabricated answer. Walk through this slowly.
Air Canada: chatbot invented a refund policy, tribunal ruled airline had to pay. NYC Lawyers: used ChatGPT for a brief, AI invented 6 fake cases, $5K fine + sanctions. Confidence does not equal accuracy.
Four strategies: 1) Ground It — upload source docs, 2) Lower Temperature — set to 0.1 for factual tasks, 3) Ask for Citations — "Quote the section where you found this", 4) The Golden Rule — human verification, never copy-paste without reading.
Four limitations: No true reasoning (simulates logic, fails at complex planning), No real-time knowledge (training cutoff), No secret keeping (don't upload PII to public models), No accountability (you are responsible for output).
Why Gemini: Multimodal native (text, images, code), Ecosystem (integrated with Google Workspace), Massive context (2M tokens). Hub-and-spoke model.
Four key areas: 1) The Prompt Bar, 2) Upload (PDFs/Excel), 3) Export to Google Docs, 4) Model Selector (Flash vs Pro). Master these tools before the lab.
Key takeaways: LLMs are prediction machines not truth machines, context windows are limited, hallucinations are a feature not a bug — verify everything. Lab: set up Gemini, do Beacon tasks, trigger a hallucination.
