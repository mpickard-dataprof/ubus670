Beacon Brand Quick Reference

Use these guidelines when writing your image generation prompts:

  • Colors: Red (#C8102E), navy, white, warm neutrals
  • Tone: Approachable, quality-focused, modern but not trendy
  • Target: Young professionals, 25-40, value quality over price
  • Style: Clean, airy, aspirational lifestyle, natural lighting

Image Prompt Writing Guide

An effective image generation prompt includes:

  1. Subject: What is the main focus? (product display, lifestyle scene, etc.)
  2. Style: What visual style? (professional photography, illustration, flat design)
  3. Composition: How is it framed? (close-up, wide shot, flat lay)
  4. Mood/Lighting: What feeling? (warm, bright, dramatic, soft)
  5. Brand elements: Colors, tone, audience signals
  6. What to avoid: Text (AI struggles with text in images), specific faces, trademarked items

Iterate and Refine (3 Rounds Minimum)

The key skill: prompt iteration. Refine your prompt based on what you learned from V1.

Pro tip: The best image prompts are specific about style (professional photography vs. illustration), composition (wide shot vs. close-up), and mood (warm vs. cool, bright vs. dramatic). Vague prompts produce generic results.
