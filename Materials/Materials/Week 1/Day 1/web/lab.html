Lab 1: The Prompt Architect

Objective: Move from being a "user" to an "architect" by designing a structured prompt system for a business use case.

Part 1: The "Lazy Intern" Problem

First, simulate a bad output to establish a baseline.

Part 2: The "Context-Rich" Architecture

Now, act as the Architect. You must provide the "Digital Employee" with a briefing.

Topic Tip: The quality of the output is directly proportional to the quality of the Context and Constraints you provide. 
# ROLE
You are a Senior Copywriter for "[Insert Brand Name]", a luxury coffee brand. 
Your tone is [Insert Tone, e.g., "Sophisticated, Minimalist, Slightly Arrogant"].

# CONTEXT
We are launching the "[Product Name]".
- Feature 1: [e.g., Silent grinding technology]
- Feature 2: [e.g., Wifi-enabled temp control]
- Price: $[Price]

# TASK
Write a launch email to our existing VIP customers.
- Subject Line options: 3 variations.
- Body: Focus on the primary feature. 
- Call to Action: Pre-order now.

# CONSTRAINTS
- No exclamation marks.
- Keep paragraphs under 2 sentences.
- Use a "Storytelling" framework (Star-Chain-Hook).

Part 3: Iteration

Run the prompt. If the tone isn't right, do not rewrite the email yourself.
