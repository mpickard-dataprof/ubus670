Dashboard Lecture Lab Quiz

Day 1 Knowledge Check

1. In the "Agentic Era" (2026), what is the primary role of the human?

Feedback: As an Architect, you design the workflows rather than just executing the typing. The AI handles the execution.

2. Which is a characteristic of "Augmentation"?

Feedback: Augmentation keeps the human in the loop to enhance quality and efficiency, whereas Automation removes the human for scalability.

3. What distinguishes a "Consultant" (useful agent) from an "Intern" (useless agent)?

Feedback: Just like a consultant needs a briefing to be effective, an AI agent needs rich context and strict constraints to deliver high-quality assets.
