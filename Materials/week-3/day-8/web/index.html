Capstone: AI Resume Screening

Days 8 & 9: Design, Build, and Compete

📅 Day 8: Wed, March 25 — Day 9: Fri, March 27 ⏱️ 2 × 4 hours 🏆 20% of course grade

Capstone Learning Objectives

  • Design a multi-agent AI pipeline with defined data contracts between agents
  • Build a 3-agent resume screening system (Parser, Evaluator, Ranker) using AI Studio and ADK
  • Evaluate real-world hiring decisions by detecting red flags, bias, and cross-resume patterns
  • Compete against classmates in a live, scored resume screening competition
  • Present your architecture, results, and lessons learned in a team video
📋

Beacon Retail Group: Seasonal Hiring

Beacon's HR team has received dozens of resumes for summer seasonal positions across 12 stores. Your team will build an AI-powered screening pipeline that parses, evaluates, and ranks every candidate — then defend your hiring recommendations in a live competition.

🚀 ~30-40 minutes

Launch Pad

Architecture blueprint, pipeline data contracts, two-phase tool workflow (AI Studio for prototyping, ADK for deployment), and competition rules. Everything you need to start building.

Build Your Pipeline

6-part guided lab: form teams, design your Parser, build your Evaluator, create your Ranker, wire the pipeline in ADK, and test with 20 practice resumes.

Start Building 🏆 Day 9: Competition Day ~120-150 minutes

Competition & Leaderboard

30 fresh resumes, 3 submission attempts, real-time leaderboard. Run your pipeline on the competition set, submit your rankings, and discover hidden patterns for bonus points.
