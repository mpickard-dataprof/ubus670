## Leadership Profiles

### Patricia "Pat" Holloway — Chief Executive Officer
- **Tenure:** 11 years (CEO since 2015)
- **Background:** Daughter of founders; MBA from Northwestern Kellogg
- **Focus:** Strategic vision, community relations, company culture
- **AI Stance:** Cautiously optimistic; wants to understand before committing

### Marcus Chen — Chief Financial Officer
- **Tenure:** 7 years
- **Background:** CPA; previously at regional accounting firm
- **Focus:** Financial planning, cost control, investor relations
- **AI Interest:** Expense automation, financial forecasting

### Sandra Torres — Chief Operating Officer
- **Tenure:** 15 years (started as store manager)
- **Background:** Promoted from within; deep retail operations expertise
- **Focus:** Store operations, supply chain, distribution
- **AI Interest:** Inventory optimization, workforce scheduling

### David Okonkwo — Chief Marketing Officer
- **Tenure:** 4 years
- **Background:** Digital marketing; previously at e-commerce startup
- **Focus:** Brand, customer experience, e-commerce, loyalty program
- **AI Interest:** Personalization, customer service automation

### Angela Reyes — Chief Human Resources Officer
- **Tenure:** 6 years
- **Background:** HR leadership at healthcare system
- **Focus:** Talent acquisition, training, employee engagement
- **AI Interest:** Recruiting efficiency, but concerned about bias

### James Park — Chief Information Officer
- **Tenure:** 3 years
- **Background:** IT leadership at regional bank
- **Focus:** Technology infrastructure, cybersecurity, digital transformation
- **AI Interest:** Technical feasibility, security implications, integration
