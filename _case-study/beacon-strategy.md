# Beacon Retail Group
## Strategic Plan 2025-2027 (Executive Summary)

*Presented to the Board of Directors — January 2025*

---

## Strategic Vision

**"By 2027, Beacon Retail Group will be the most trusted regional retailer in the Midwest, known for exceptional customer experiences powered by smart technology and delivered by engaged employees."**

---

## Strategic Context

### External Environment

**Opportunities:**
- Growing consumer preference for local/regional retailers
- AI and automation technologies becoming accessible to mid-market companies
- Competitors slow to personalize customer experiences
- Strong regional economy and population growth in target markets

**Threats:**
- Amazon and national chains continue to gain market share
- Rising labor costs and tight labor market
- Consumer expectations set by digital-native retailers
- Economic uncertainty and inflation pressures

### Internal Assessment

**Strengths:**
- Strong brand recognition in core markets
- Loyal customer base (245K loyalty members)
- Experienced leadership team
- Debt-free balance sheet

**Weaknesses:**
- Aging technology infrastructure
- Below-industry customer satisfaction scores
- High employee turnover, especially seasonal
- Limited data analytics capabilities
- E-commerce platform needs modernization

### Pillar 2: Workforce Excellence

**Objective:** Attract, develop, and retain the best retail talent in our markets.

**Key Initiatives:**

| Initiative | Timeline | Investment | Expected Impact |
|------------|----------|------------|-----------------|
| Hiring Process Redesign | Q1 2026 | $50K | Reduce time-to-fill by 25% |
| Seasonal Hiring Optimization | Q3 2026 | TBD | Reduce seasonal turnover to 25% |

## AI Strategy — Task Force Charter

As part of our Digital Transformation pillar, we are establishing an **AI Strategy Task Force** to:

1. **Assess** AI readiness across three priority areas:
   - Seasonal hiring (HR)
   - Customer service (Marketing)
   - Expense processing (Finance)

2. **Prototype** proof-of-concept solutions using available AI tools

3. **Recommend** which initiatives to pursue, defer, or decline

4. **Establish** governance principles for responsible AI use

**Task Force Timeline:** March 2026 (3 weeks)

**Investment:** $150K allocated for AI pilots; additional funding available if justified

**Success Criteria:**
- At least one prototype demonstrates measurable value
- Clear recommendation on AI roadmap for 2026-2027
- Governance framework established before any deployment

## Risk Factors

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Economic downturn | Medium | High | Maintain cash reserves; flexible cost structure |
| Technology project delays | Medium | Medium | Phased implementation; vendor SLAs |
| Labor market tightens further | High | Medium | Competitive wages; culture investment |
| AI initiatives underperform | Medium | Low | Limited initial investment; clear ROI gates |
| Competitor AI adoption | High | Medium | Stay informed; fast-follower strategy |

## Governance

## Conclusion

Beacon Retail Group has a solid foundation and a clear path forward. Our strategy balances prudent investment in technology and people with disciplined financial management.

The AI Strategy Task Force represents a measured approach to emerging technology—we will learn, experiment, and make evidence-based decisions rather than chasing hype.

Our goal is not to become a technology company, but to use technology wisely to serve our customers and communities better.
